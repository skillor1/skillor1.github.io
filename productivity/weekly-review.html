üìñ How to Use Weekly Review

Transform scattered daily activities into strategic progress! Weekly Review helps you reflect on accomplishments, identify blockers, and set clear priorities for the coming week. Generate AI-powered insights from your reflections and export professional PDF reports to track long-term growth.

1 Reflect on What Went Well

In the first text area, write about your week's accomplishments, wins (big or small), and positive moments. Include completed projects, skills learned, relationships strengthened, habits maintained. Be specific‚Äî"Finished React project 3 days early" beats "Had a good week."

2 Identify Areas to Improve

In the second text area, honestly assess challenges, missed goals, and areas needing attention next week. This isn't about self-criticism‚Äîit's about strategic problem-solving. Example: "Interrupted focus sessions 5 times daily ‚Üí need to block calendar better."

3 Generate AI Insights

Click "Generate Review & Insights" to analyze your reflections. AI extracts keywords from both sections and provides 4-5 actionable insights (e.g., "Your top strengths this week were coding, consistency. Leverage these next week." + "Biggest opportunity: addressing time management.").

4 Export & Track Progress

Click "Export as PDF" to download a formatted report with date stamp, reflections, AI insights, and next week action plan. Save PDFs monthly to create a progress timeline. Review Stats panel tracks total reviews completed, insights generated, and PDFs exported for accountability.

Understanding AI-Generated Insights:

How AI Analysis Works:

  • Keyword Extraction: AI identifies frequent words in your reflections (e.g., "coding," "exercise," "meetings")
  • Pattern Recognition: Compares "went well" vs "to improve" keywords to spot strengths vs weaknesses
  • Insight Generation: Creates 4-5 actionable recommendations based on keyword frequency and sentiment
  • Example: If "coding" appears 5x in wins but "time management" appears 3x in challenges ‚Üí insight suggests leveraging coding strength while improving time management

4 Types of AI Insights You'll See:

  1. Strengths Recognition: "Your top strengths this week were X, Y, Z. Leverage these next week."
  2. Growth Opportunities: "Areas for growth include A, B. Consider setting specific goals to address these."
  3. Consistency Praise: "You demonstrated consistency in [keyword]. Building on this momentum will help next week."
  4. Priority Guidance: "The biggest opportunity for next week is addressing [top challenge keyword]."

How to Turn Insights into Actionable Priorities:

Step 1: Categorize Insights (Do-Continue-Stop-Start Framework):

  • Do More: Strengths identified by AI (e.g., "coding" = strength ‚Üí allocate MORE time to coding next week)
  • Continue: Habits/routines working well (e.g., "consistency in exercise" ‚Üí maintain 6x/week)
  • Stop: Challenges identified (e.g., "too many meetings" ‚Üí decline non-essential meetings)
  • Start: New behaviors to address gaps (e.g., "time management" issue ‚Üí start time-blocking)

Step 2: Apply the "Rule of 3" for Priorities:

  • Why 3? Brain can focus on max 3-5 priorities weekly without overwhelm
  • Priority 1 (Work/Career): Based on biggest AI insight from "went well" section
  • Priority 2 (Personal Growth): Based on top "to improve" challenge
  • Priority 3 (Health/Relationships): Balance‚Äîdon't neglect these for work
