Everything you need to know about Skillor's productivity tools

Skillor is a comprehensive platform offering 50+ free productivity and learning tools for professionals. We provide tools across multiple categories including Typing Practice, Programming (JSON formatter, Regex tester, API mock), Aptitude Tests, English Learning (dictation, vocabulary), Career Tools (resume builder, salary calculator), AI Tools, Design Tools, and Productivity Boosters. All tools are browser-based, require no installation, and are completely free to use.

Yes! All Skillor tools are 100% free with no hidden costs, subscriptions, or paywalls. We believe in making professional development and productivity tools accessible to everyone. You can use unlimited sessions, practice as much as you want, and access all features without creating an account or providing payment information.

No account creation is required! Most Skillor tools work instantly without any sign-up. However, some tools use browser localStorage to save your progress locally (like typing practice results or job application tracking). This data stays on your device for your convenience, but no personal information is collected or sent to our servers.

For interview prep, our most popular tools include: Typing Speed Test (improve typing efficiency), Interview Vocabulary Quiz (master professional English), Mock Interview Practice (with AI feedback), Aptitude Test Tools (data interpretation, reasoning), Resume Builder, Salary Calculator (compare job offers), and Programming Tools (for technical interviews). These tools have helped thousands of users successfully prepare for job interviews.

Absolutely! All Skillor tools are fully responsive and mobile-friendly. They work seamlessly across smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. The interface automatically adapts to your screen size for the best experience. Some typing tools work better on physical keyboards, but most tools including AI tools, calculators, and learning resources work perfectly on touch devices.

Our AI tools (like Caption Generator, Hashtag Generator, Email Responder, and Meme Creator) use advanced natural language processing to generate professional content. Simply input your requirements, and the tool generates high-quality outputs instantly. For example, the Email Responder analyzes incoming emails and suggests appropriate professional responses, saving you time while maintaining quality communication.

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities. Most tools process data entirely in your browser without sending it to our servers. When local storage is used (for saving progress), data stays on your device only. We don't collect, store, or share personal information. No tracking cookies are used, and we comply with all data protection regulations. Read our Privacy Policy for complete details.
