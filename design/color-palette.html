Create harmonious color schemes instantly with AI-powered mood detection and color theory-based harmony types:

1 Generate or Choose Mood

Click "Random Palette" for instant colors, or type a mood (sunset, ocean, corporate, neon) and press Enter. AI generates matching color schemes based on your keywords.

2 Select Harmony Type

Choose Complementary (contrasting), Analogous (similar hues), Triadic (evenly spaced), or Monochromatic (same hue, different shades). Each creates different vibes.

3 Copy Colors or Code

Click any color swatch to copy HEX code. Export as CSS variables, RGB values, or Tailwind config. Save favorite palettes for later use.

4 Save and Reuse

Click "Save Palette" to store favorites locally. Load saved palettes anytime to maintain brand consistency across projects.
