üìñ How to Use Email Responder

Craft professional email responses in seconds with AI assistance. Perfect for busy professionals who need quick, well-written replies:

1 Paste the Original Email

Copy and paste the email you received into the input box. The AI will analyze the tone, urgency, and key points to generate an appropriate response.

2 Choose Response Tone

Select from Professional, Friendly, Formal, Casual, or Apologetic tones based on your relationship with the recipient and context of the conversation.

3 Select Template (Optional)

Pick a pre-made template for common scenarios like meeting requests, thank you notes, follow-ups, or rejections to speed up the process.

4 Generate and Edit

Click "Generate Response" to create your reply. Review, customize if needed, and copy to your email client. Build conversation chains for ongoing threads.
