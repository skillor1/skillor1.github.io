üìñ How to Use Resume Builder

Create professional, ATS-optimized resumes with real-time scoring and keyword recommendations from job descriptions:

1 Add Your Information

Fill personal details, summary, and add sections for experience, education, and skills. Click "+" buttons to add multiple entries. Remove items with "Remove" buttons.

2 Paste Job Description (Optional)

Paste target job description in the field provided. The ATS scorer will match your skills against job requirements and suggest keywords to add.

3 Check ATS Score

Click "Generate Resume" to see live preview and ATS score (0-100). Aim for 80+ score. Follow recommendations to improve keyword matching and formatting.

4 Download Resume

Download as HTML (opens in any browser, easy to convert) or PDF (ready to send). ATS-friendly formatting ensures your resume passes automated screening.
