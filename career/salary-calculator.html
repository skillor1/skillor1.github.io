üìñ How to Use Salary Calculator

Get accurate Indian market salary ranges by role, city, and company type, plus compare multiple job offers side-by-side:

1 Choose Calculation Mode

Select "Single Salary" to check market rates for your role, or "Offer Comparison" to compare 2+ job offers with CTC breakdown and location adjustment.

2 Enter Job Details

For single mode: select role, experience, company size, and city. For comparison: add multiple offers with CTC, fixed/variable split, stocks, and location.

3 View Salary Breakdown

See expected salary range, monthly take-home, PF contributions, and tax estimates. For comparisons, winner is marked based on adjusted value (accounting for cost of living).

4 Make Informed Decisions

Use insights for salary negotiations or job offer decisions. Remember: highest CTC doesn't always mean highest take-home after location costs.
