üìñ How to Use Interview Prep

Practice real interview questions with timer, voice recording, and AI-powered feedback to ace your next interview:

1 Select Your Role

Choose your target role (SDE, PM, Marketing, etc.) to get relevant interview questions. Each role has 8+ curated behavioral and technical questions.

2 Record Your Answer

Click "Start Recording" and answer as if in a real interview. The 2-minute timer helps you practice concise answers. Pause timer if you need more time.

3 Review Your Performance

Play back your recording. Check AI feedback for filler word count and speaking speed. Self-rate your answer from 1-5 stars.

4 Track 30-Day Challenge

Practice daily to complete the 30-day interview prep challenge. Consistent practice builds confidence and reduces interview anxiety.
